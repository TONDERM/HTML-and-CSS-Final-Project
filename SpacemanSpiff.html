Spaceman Spiff
Spaceman Spiff is another creation of Calvin's imagination and one of his most prominent alter egos. He is a space explorer who often does battle with aliens, either on foot or in space. The events in his daydreaming are usually events that are happening outside of his imagination, such as his teacher talking or other kids persuading him to slide down a slide. The colorful, exotic planets and aliens are some of my favorites of the colored strips. I especially enjoy the names of the aliens the adventurer encounters.
